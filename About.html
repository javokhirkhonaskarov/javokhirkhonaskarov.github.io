About Me

Hi! I'm Javo Askarov — a licensed real estate agent with Corcoran in New York City and a senior at Arizona State University, majoring in Business.

Working in NYC real estate allows me to help people navigate one of the most exciting and competitive housing markets in the world. I specialize in rentals and apartments across Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens, and I love helping clients find the perfect place that truly fits their lifestyle.

I created Explore NYC to break down neighborhoods, explain iconic buildings, and simplify the search process for anyone trying to understand the real NYC — not just the version on TV. My goal is to make learning about the city exciting, easy, and stress-free.

Whether you're apartment hunting, researching areas, or just love New York, I'm always happy to help answer questions and guide you every step of the way.
